Who am I?

As good a question as any! I'm a fourth year student at UBC Okanagan studying computer science and psychology. Never content to stop learning, I thrive in dynamic environments that allow me to push my capabilities to the limit. I pick up technical knowledge rapidly and apply it to complex problems effectively, while retaining a touch for soft skills that helps me be an great collaborator and leader.

To that end, I'm currently a Menon Fellow in Menon Lab's inaugural cohort, where 1 in 17 applicants were selected to become a Fellow. There, I'm working with a team of talented students as a frontend developer for Project Access where we are building a next generation mentorship platform for underprivileged students looking to attend a top tier university.
